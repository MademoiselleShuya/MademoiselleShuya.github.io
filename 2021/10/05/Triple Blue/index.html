Triple Blue
Date: 19/07/2016, Photographer: Miss S, Post-Processing: Science Cookie

Hello, I am here to annoy you again, as I found a perfect excuse to update my website. I would like to use markdown to write my Ph.D thesis and I watched a Youtube video which is a quick introduction of markdown. For practice, I am trying to write my blogs using markdown.

In that summer we had a tour around Taiwan, and that was the craziest trip ever in my life. Before we rode a motorbike along the coastline, our rider Dr. Crystal had only 2 hours training, maybe even less than that. Luckily, we survived! It was a fantastic ride on a sunny afternoon, although I got a severe sunburn to the point my skin started peeling. With fishing villages in the background and the wind blowing past us we sang our hearts out on Mayday rock songs. The picture below was captured when we were lying on the beach soaking in the sunshine. The ground, the ocean and the sky are connected in a soft blue utopia. As a baby biologist I truly believe blue is the color of life, and the ocean is the place where we come from.
