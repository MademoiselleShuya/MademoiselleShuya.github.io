Healing
Date: 22/11/2021, Photographer: Miss S, Post-Processing: Science Cookie

Hey ya,

It’s been a while since the last time I updated my blog. I’ve been quite emotional lately, and I haven’t felt like doing anything. I abandoned my self-growth to play mobile games every day. A pro-gaming lifestyle without the skills to back it up. Often end up losing ten games in a row, a bit awkward though…

Over the last few weeks, I spent most of my daytime goofing off, and my nighttime feeling deeply regretful being a goofball… only to do it again the next day. As a Ph.D. candidate, I’ve got a lot of balls to juggle, from writing up my thesis to looking for a viable job position. The pressure is on and my pot is boiling over. Physical side effects like headache and stomachache soon followed my psychological distress. Our bodies and minds are not separate, so it’s not surprising that mental ill-health leads to physical ill-health.

Luckily, we humans have friends and family to call upon to lift us from our seemingly eternal internal nightmares. Aided by those around me, I am setting out a rescue plan: sleeping early, eating well, and doing more exercise. The book The Yellow Emperor’s Canon of Internal Medicine will accompany me through my rescue.

Honestly, this photograph has nothing to do with my article. I just enjoy touching up images when I am blue. I chose this sunset to display, as it can be related to the word healing. This moment was captured on my way to Edinburgh. This enchanting view gifted to me at that moment made me grateful to be here on Earth. I appreciate everything I have been through. What Does Not Kill Me Makes Me Stronger!

Ciao ciao!
