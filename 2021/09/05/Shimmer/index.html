Shimmer
Date: 03/08/2021, Photographer: Miss S, Post-Processing: little青葱

This photograph was taken on a lovely afternoon when I was in a rush to visit the Museum of Classical Archaeology. It is one of the most famous checkout points at Cambridge, known as Mathematical Bridge. All of the famed Cambridge attributes are included in this picture: Bridge, River Cam, and Punting. The stunning view reminds me a poem Saying Good-bye to Cambridge Again written by the great savant, Zhimo Xu. In that moment, I felt I truly understood what he meant when he said:

Their reflections on the shimmering waves

Always linger in the depth of my heart.
