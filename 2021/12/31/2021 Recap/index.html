2021 Recap
Date: 05/06/2021, Photographer: Miss S, Post-Processing: Science Cookie

Hey,

In this post, I was initially thinking about going over my 2021. However, as I sat down to reflect, my mind went blank with where to start. I have nothing to recap!

Contrary to what you might think, the reasoning behind this was everything felt so special on its own wierd kind of way ;). This year everything felt so quick yet slow enough live every moment, dull yet exciting enough to smile back and laugh, difficult yet rewarding enough to keep going. Chaotic you might say, I know but in all the chaos I found peace standing at the center of the storm.

As always, I started the year by setting out targets, some naturally overachieving ones. Despite their seeming hardness, I can proudly say I completed 80% of my 2021 targets and sprinkled in some unexpected achievements too.

Overall, I had a great year :).

So, seeee yaaaa 2021.

HAPPY NEW YEAR

Music for the last day of the year: Nocturnes, Op. 9: No. 1 in B-Flat Minor
